# strakez/ddev-custom
A collection of custom DDEV commands and settings

## Disclaimer

The included scripts and commands were carefully prompted using Gemini to produce the desired output. I have since manually refined, adjusted, and tested them to ensure they do exact what is intended (and none of what isn't).

I understand that AI can be a great tool for generating content, however, we must maintain the responsibility for what is produced, just as a manufacturer is responsibile for what their CNC tools produce. That being said, scripts and settings in the experimental folder still require refinement, but are supplied as-is for those who wish to do their own refinement.
